1. Scope

This policy describes privacy practices for the mobile application Christmas Decor Game distributed on Apple’s App Store. It applies to use of the app on supported iPhone, iPad, and compatible Apple devices. It does not govern other products or services unless we say so in a future update here.

2. Data collection

According to the App Privacy details shown on the App Store listing for Christmas Decor Game, the developer has indicated that this app does not collect data from you for the categories Apple requires developers to disclose there.

Store labels can change when the app is updated. For the latest disclosure, open the app’s page on the App Store and review the App Privacy section.

3. Apple and the App Store

Installing, updating, or purchasing through the App Store involves processing by Apple Inc. under Apple’s own terms and privacy policy. That includes account, billing, and device-related processing that Apple controls. We do not operate those systems.

4. Information on your device

The game may store local data on your device—such as level progress, preferences, or similar settings—so gameplay works without requiring a constant network connection. This information ordinarily remains on your device unless a future version explicitly syncs or transmits it; any change would be reflected in App Store release notes and privacy labeling where applicable.

5. Advertising

The App Store age and content information for this app notes that it contains advertising. Ad networks and partners, if shown inside the app, may use their own technologies subject to their policies and to platform rules. Review in-app experiences and system privacy settings (including Apple’s tracking and advertising controls) if you want to limit related activity.

6. Children and age rating

The app is rated 4+ on the App Store. Parents and guardians can use Apple’s Screen Time, Family Sharing, and purchase controls to manage installs and spending. This policy is written in plain language; younger users should review it with a responsible adult.

7. Updates to this policy

We may revise this page to keep it accurate or to match changes to the app or platform requirements. When we do, we will update the Last updated date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the revised policy, except where applicable law requires different steps.

Christmas Decor Game

Festive hidden object fun

Free Puzzle iPhone & iPad 4+

Step into a magical holiday world filled with beautifully decorated Christmas scenes. Christmas Decor Game is a hidden object adventure: search each scene, spot every item on your list, and finish before the timer runs out. Earn stars, unlock new levels, and use hints when a tricky decoration hides in plain sight.

How you play

  • Explore rich, festive rooms and winter tableaux packed with detail.
  • Find every hidden object before time runs out to clear the level.
  • Chase high scores with the star rating—speed and accuracy matter.
  • Use hints when you are stuck; difficulty grows as you progress.

Why players love it

  • Stunning Christmas-themed art—cozy lights, ornaments, and winter charm.
  • Timer-based challenges that reward sharp eyes and calm focus.
  • Smooth, simple controls—tap to find; perfect for quick sessions.
  • Progressive difficulty—each level adds new surprises to spot.
  • Relaxing for a quiet evening or exciting when you race the clock—your choice.
